Dark Souls Series

Dark souls series or as fans like to call them "Souls born games" are action role-playing games that are played in 3rd person.

The most appealing thing about these games is their difficulty and how can you overcome the challenges they present, this is also a birth place of Git gud meme. Something is tough you just need to get better. This games doesn't hold your hand, you need to figure stuff on your own.

In the game as you start you are promted to choose from few classes, those can range from melee classes to magical ones.
