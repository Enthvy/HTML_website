Call of Duty Franchise

To start the list off we have Call of duty franchise

I chose the COD franchise as my No.1 because it stuck with me throughout the years. It's a game I love to come back to no matter the circumstances.

I don't have a one game that I love the most but, the closes one to that would be Call of Duty Black ops 3.

Small description of COD. COD is a first person shooter which has single player and multiplayer elements.

Usually the mainline COD games consist of:

  • Campaign
  • Multiplayer
  • Special 3rd Mode, can be zombies, special ops.

Campaign click me for images

Campaign is usually set in a time period of the theme the game is set in, but can deviate from it. New COD games tend to break this rule.

There is a story that is told in a war setting.

Most people don't play the Campaign because they only play the multiplayer element. Which leads us to :

Multiplayer click me for images

Multiplayer is the reason why most of the playerbase buys the game and is the most played gamemode.

In this mode you can faceoff other players all over the world, depending on your internet connection this can be a good experience or a bad one.

New call of duty games use a feature called SBM "Skill based match making " that in theory should help new playerd find same skill level players, but all it does is that it makes the game harder when you start to win.

Lot's of players hate this feature and are fighting the developers for it's removal.

3rd Mode click me for images

This one can be Zombies, (my favourite) or Special OPS.

For Zombies it mainly consists of a wave based experience where you fight off the horde of undead wave after wave.

You can play this gamemode with up to 4 players in game. Over the years this mode evolved from simple shoot your way through the zombies into more emersive gameplay experience involving solving easter eggs and doing puzzles and fighting game Bosses.

Overal the games from this series are good but there are bad ones too, but even tho it's almost the same game without changing any formulas I love to come back each year for new experience.
